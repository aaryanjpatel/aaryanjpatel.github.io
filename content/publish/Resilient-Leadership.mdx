Preparing for selective entry exams requires a structured approach across all subjects. This guide provides strategies, resources, and tips to help students excel in 2026.

## 📚 Subjects Covered

- **Mathematics**: Problem-solving, algebra, geometry, and advanced topics.
- **English**: Reading comprehension, essay writing, and vocabulary.
- **Science**: Physics, chemistry, biology, and scientific reasoning.
- **General Ability**: Logical reasoning, pattern recognition, and critical thinking.

## 📝 Study Plan

### 1. Mathematics
Focus on mastering core concepts and practicing past exam papers. Use online resources like Khan Academy and selective entry preparation books.

### 2. English
Improve reading comprehension by analyzing articles and essays. Practice writing under timed conditions to enhance clarity and structure.

### 3. Science
Conduct experiments and review foundational concepts. Use interactive tools like simulations to understand complex topics.

### 4. General Ability
Solve puzzles and logical reasoning questions daily. Familiarize yourself with common question types in selective entry exams.

## 🔑 Key Strategies

- **Time Management**: Allocate specific time slots for each subject.
- **Mock Exams**: Simulate exam conditions to build confidence.
- **Review Mistakes**: Analyze errors to avoid repeating them.
- **Seek Help**: Join study groups or hire a tutor for challenging areas.

## 📘 Resources

- **Books**: Selective entry preparation guides for each subject.
- **Online Platforms**: Websites and apps offering practice questions and tutorials.
- **Workshops**: Attend seminars and coaching sessions tailored for selective entry exams.

## 🏆 Success Tips

- Stay consistent with your study schedule.
- Take regular breaks to avoid burnout.
- Stay positive and believe in your abilities.

> Success in selective entry exams comes from a combination of hard work, smart strategies, and self-confidence. Start preparing today to achieve your goals in 2026.
